NAME
	/me - make your avatar pretend to "do" something in the room

SYNOPSIS
	/me &lt;string&gt;

EXAMPLE
	/me gently cries himself to sleep.

REMARKS
	Use this command to pretend that your user is doing something in the room.
	Refer to this informative article on pretending for more information:
	http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Role-playing
